Security

We, at P8 Poker, take our customers’ enjoyment of poker very seriously and any form of fraudulent activity, as determined by and at The Company’s sole discretion, is strictly prohibited.

Collusion is a form of cheating in which two or more players either secretly exchange hole card information or otherwise form a cheating partnership to the detriment of other players sitting at the same table. This includes but is not limited to ‘chip dumping’, ‘squeezing’, ‘stack balancing’ and advising/agreeing to take specific actions with other players through personal messages.

Our system monitors card history, betting patterns, player history and their gameplay, including all bets, for all players, up to and including the last round of betting on the river card.

Where we suspect that players have attempted or committed collusion, their accounts will be immediately suspended and any remaining chips from their accounts will be confiscated. This may include related accounts that we deem connected and/or involved in suspicious or abnormal activities.

In all cases, P8 Poker’s decision will be final and no correspondence will be entered into.
